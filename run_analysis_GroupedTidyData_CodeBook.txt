Data Dictionary - Averages of mean and standard deviation based on Activity and Subject
"Activity"
	Description: Defines activities performed by volunteers
	Data Type: Character String
	Values: WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING
"Subject"
	Description: Categorizes volunteers into different buckets based on the activities they perform
	Data Type: Integer
	Values: 1 to 30
